United States Flow Map family Tree

Areas that are receiving more families are many of the rural areas. Texas, Tennessee, South Dakota, Wyoming, and Arizona. These areas are most likely receiving more flow because people are spreading out from the East and looking for more farming opportunities. The general flow directions are coming from the East to the West and North to South. We can see that the darker green on the basemap is representing the total populations, darker being more and lighter being less. The circle size is also represented by incoming flow, the larger circles have more flow. The arrows of flow represent which way the flow is moving to and from and the size of the arrow is representing the size of the flow from the origins.
